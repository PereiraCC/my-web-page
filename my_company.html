My management

This project simulates the management of a small company in which it has agents, products and users.

Tech Stack

Mobile app

In the mobile application you have the possibility to register with our email and password or enter with your Google account.
You can also create your company's agents where you can attach your basic information such as name, phone numbers, email, and even include a profile photo.
You can do the same with your products from your company where you can attach your basic information such as name, price, and photo.
Soon it will be in the Play Store if you want to see the source code here is the repository.
